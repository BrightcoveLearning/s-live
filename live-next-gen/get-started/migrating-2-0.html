Using the UI

Navigating to Live 2.0

  1. Navigate to theLive Module
  2. Click Switch to Live 2.0

Create a New Job

  1. Navigate to theLive Module
  2. Click + New Live Channel

  3. Fill your Live Channel information

    For more information about configuring your Live Channel, visit the "Creating a new event" and "Configuring advanced options" sections of This document
  4. Click Save
  5. Find your Encoder source URL

    You can find your Source URL once your Channel is Live. It can take a few minutes for the Channel to Start.
